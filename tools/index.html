Design Thinking Tools
1. Unit Economics
Estimate CAC, LTV, and COGS for your venture. Copy the output when you're done — you'll paste it into the Venture Assessment.
2. Market Sizing
Estimate your TAM, SAM, and year-one target. Copy the output when you're done — you'll paste it into the Venture Assessment.
3. Financial Model
Build a 3-year financial model from your unit economics and market sizing. Walks you through fixed costs, variable costs, and growth assumptions, then produces a brief with sensitivity analysis and the top things to pressure-test.
4. Venture Assessment
Walk through Desirability, Feasibility, and Viability. Includes Unit Economics and Market Sizing inline if you haven't run them yet. Produces your final assessment brief and presentation deck.
